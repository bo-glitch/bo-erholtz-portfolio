About

I’m interested in roles where analytics, strategy, and growth intersect.

I enjoy work that starts with ambiguity: understanding the question, finding the right data, identifying patterns, and translating analysis into a clear business recommendation. My experience includes corporate marketing strategy, competitive intelligence, market research, business modeling, and early-stage venture planning.

I work best on problems that require both structure and curiosity: sizing an opportunity, comparing markets, understanding customer behavior, or building a recommendation from imperfect information.
