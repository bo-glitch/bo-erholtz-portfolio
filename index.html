About

I turn ambiguity into direction.

I'm a senior at the Carlson School of Management graduating this May, triple majoring in Marketing, Business Analytics, and Entrepreneurial Management. I pay attention to how things work, where friction exists, and how decisions can be made better through data, research, and clear thinking. I do my best work in environments with high standards, strong teams, and a constant push to improve.

I'm especially interested in the work that happens before decisions get made — digging into data, noticing patterns, asking better questions, and understanding what is really driving results. I enjoy ambiguous problems, building structure where there isn't any, and finding better ways to operate, grow, and innovate.
